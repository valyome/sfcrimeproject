Charts

Larceny

This is by far the mot common crime in th last 12 years accounting for about 20% of all crimes. Looking at the data one can see a big increase in theft in the end of 2011. Again, the data points are rolling averages of every 50 days.

Vehicle Theft

Vehicle theft, albeit only about 6% of all crimes done in the last 12 years has had a significant drop in the span between june 2005 and october 2006. At first we hypotesized this may be corelated with the increase in SFPD force but this report on Kaggle showed us that the SFPD has actually stopped putting recovered vehicles in that category so this graph would be misleading.

Assaults

Assaults have been steadily around the 35 per day average for the past 12 years.

Non-criminal

This is a interesting category. It includes primarily lost property reports, mental crisis aid from the SFPD, death reports and found property reports. THere is a big increase from 2009 to 2013 indicating more significantly more people started reporting to the police force.

Vandalism

Judging by the graph below vandalism has been a very steady and common crime category for the entire span of the 12 years.
